Why YouTube Is A Trap

YouTube uses an algorithm, information overload, and the power of the endless scroll to turn itself into a supposed virutal paradise where the user loses track of time. However, this supposed paradise can have negative impacts on your mental health.

YouTube uses an algorithm and other tricks to turn itself into a trap. Why YouTube Is A Trap discusses these tricks and how to thwart them.

The Steam Tunnels of Virginia Tech

There is a world of steaming labyrinths right below your feet, and you probably do not even know about it. This world is the steam tunnels.

The steam tunnels are a vast underground labyrinth that intrigues Virginia Tech students with its history and dangers. In The Steam Tunnels of Virginia Tech, Virginia Tech Students will enjoy hearing stories of the steam tunnels.

The Rules of Quadball(Quidditch)

If you have ever read the Harry Potter books or watched the movies, you might have thought to yourself, “Quidditch sounds really fun, I wonder how it would work in real life?” Well wonder no more, because quidditch, now known as quadball, is a real sport and in this video I will give you a basic rundown of how it works.

Quadball is a full contact, co-ed sport that is based on quidditch from the Harry Potter books. In The Rules of Quadball(Quidditch), viewers will get a quick overview of how the game is played.
